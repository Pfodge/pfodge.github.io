A Very Random Ocarina of Time Birthday Challenge

A Little Bit of Pre-Race Setup

Just a quick thing before the race can begin.

1. Run SoH and press Esc to bring up the menu

2.Click on Settings then click on Presets

3.Look for Enhancements - Curated Randomizer and click on Apply next to it

NB: When you want to go back to playing the game normally I'd go back to this menu and apply Enhancements - Curated First Time Vanilla

Joining the Race

Please get your joining information from the Teams page first

1. Start SoH and select an empty file

2. Select "Archipelago" as your quest

3. Go to "Change Connection Info" and enter your Server Address and Slot Name in the appropriate places

4.Close the "Archipelago Settings" window and hit "Connect and Start Archipelago"

5.You should be Connected and start playing
