Installing Ship of Harkinian (SoH)

I've got guides for Windows and MacOS, but if you've already got it set up you can skip this

  1. Download SoH from this link
  2. Extract the downloaded zip file into a folder somewhere convenient that you'll remember where it is
  3. Download this file into the folder where you extracted the file from step 2
  4. Go to the folder and run soh.exe
  5. When it shows up with "No O2R Files", click Yes
  6. On the window that opens up, locate the "LozOot.z64" file you downloaded in step 3 and Open it
  7. Wait for ROM Extraction to complete
  8. When it says "Extraction Complete", click No
  9. SoH is now installed and should start
  10. Press Esc in SoH to open settings
  11. Click on Graphics
  12. Click on Toggle Fullscreen (if want to play fullscreen)
  13. click on the checkbox next to "Match Refresh Rate".
  14. You'll also want to click on Controls and configure them
    • A is the action/interact button, and also plays a note when you have your ocarina out
    • B is sword attacks
    • C [directions] and D[directions] are for using items. C [directions] also play notes when you have your ocarina out
    • R is for your shield
    • Z is targetting lock-on
    • C Up is to look around in first-person, respond to Navi when says "Hey", and to play a note when you have your ocarina out
    • Start is to pause and access your inventory, maps, songs, and other info
  15. If you are just using mouse and/or keyboard: click Controls, scroll down, click "Camera Controls", and then click "Enable Mouse Controls" to use your mouse when you're aiming the slingshot, bow, or hookshot
  16. Make a shortcut of or remember where soh.exe is because that's how you start the game
  17. Enjoy! Might be worth playing a little a bit of the game to get a feel for it, although be aware the race will be a very different experience
