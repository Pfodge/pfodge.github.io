A Little Bit of Pre-Race Setup

Just a quick thing before the race can begin.

  1. Run SoH and press Esc to bring up the menu
  2. Click on Settings then click on Presets
  3. Look for Enhancements - Curated Randomizer and click on Apply next to it

NB: When you want to go back to playing the game normally I'd go back to this menu and apply Enhancements - Curated First Time Vanilla

Joining the Race

  1. Start SoH and select an empty file

  2. Select "Archipelago" as your quest

  3. Go to "Change Connection Info" and enter your Server Address and Slot Name in the appropriate places

  4. Close the "Archipelago Settings" window and hit "Connect and Start Archipelago"

  5. You should be Connected and start playing

Some advice for setting up your controls
  1. When SoH is running, press ESC to open the menu
  2. Click on Controls
  3. Here's the controls you'll need to assign:
    • A is the action/interact button, and also plays a note when you have your ocarina out
    • B is sword attacks
    • C←,C↓,C→ and D←,D↓,D→,D↑ are for using items
    • R is for your shield
    • Z is targetting lock-on, and also moves the camera behind you
    • C↑ is to look around in first-person, respond to Navi when says "Hey"
    • C←,C↓,C→,C↑, and A also play notes when you have your ocarina out
    • Start is to pause and access your inventory, maps, songs, and other info
  4. If you are using a keyboard: scroll down, click "Camera Controls", and then click "Enable Mouse Controls" to use your mouse when you're aiming the slingshot, bow, or hookshot. The keyboard aiming controls are too sensitive
