A Very Random Ocarina of Time Birthday Challenge

This is a race between two teams to finish a Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Randomiser Multiworld

A Randomiser mixes up the items you receive in the game randomly to allow you to experience it differently than a normal playthrough

A Multiworld is a type of online multiplayer where 2 or more players have worlds that are linked together. This means that some of the items are randomised between the players’ worlds. How this works is:

  1. Player A might open a chest and find Player B’s bow. Player A can’t use it
  2. Instead, the bow gets sent to Player B’s world so they suddenly have a bow they can use to find more items
  3. This continues until all players have found all items needed to finish the game, which is: defeating Ganondorf at his castleh

Note: Multiworlds are asynchronous. This means that you can start and stop playing whenever you want. If somebody else finds items in their game for you while you’re away, they’ll be waiting for you when you start playing again. Just be sure to make some progress when you can otherwise your teammates might get stuck
